About us

At Theo’s Automotive Repairs, we offer a wide range of auto mechanical repairs, maintenance and services. Our team can handle brake and clutch repairs, wheel alignments, tyre servicing, exhaust systems, and so much more. Let our team look after your vehicle with our engine reconditioning, logbook servicing and car inspection services. Located in Warilla, our mechanics provide reliable and professional auto mechanical work. We use high-quality equipment to accurately diagnose your vehicle. Our team is committed to ensuring your car is running smoothly. For your convenience, we also offer pick-up and delivery services. Contact us today to book a service!
